Claude Code, for the way social scientists actually work. Sosyal bilimcinin gercekte calistigi bicimde, Claude Code.

A bilingual, open-source guide to using Anthropic's Claude Code in research, teaching, and academic writing. Written by a working clinical psychologist and PhD candidate, with verified citations, full Turkish and English parity, and auditable AI disclosure in every booklet. Her broşürde dogrulanmis atiflar, tam Turkce ve Ingilizce parite ve denetlenebilir yapay zeka ifsasi.

Why this guideNeden bu rehber

Honest about the value and the limits of agentic AI.Ajansal yapay zekanin hem degerine hem sinirina karsi durust.

Not a Claude Code reference manual, and not a promotion document. Three commitments separate it from comparable guides.Ne bir Claude Code referans kilavuzu, ne de bir tanitim belgesi. Uc taahhut onu benzer rehberlerden ayirir.

01

Full Turkish and English parityTam Turkce ve Ingilizce parite

The Turkish version is written first by a native speaker. The English version is re-authored from the same outline, not machine-translated. A continuous integration check refuses any commit that breaks the bilingual pair.Turkce surum once ana dili konusan biri tarafindan yazilir. Ingilizce surum ayni outline'dan yeniden-yazilir, makine cevirisi degildir. Bir surekli entegrasyon kontrolu, iki dilli cifti bozan her commit'i reddeder.

02

Verified citations, zero fabricatedDogrulanmis atiflar, sifir uydurma

Language models routinely fabricate references. Every DOI here is verified against Crossref and doi.org. The fabrication rate of the guide's own bibliography is a tracked, publicly reported metric. It is zero.Dil modelleri rutin olarak kaynak uydurur. Buradaki her DOI Crossref ve doi.org uzerinden dogrulanir. Rehberin kendi kaynakcasinin uydurma orani izlenen, herkese acik raporlanan bir olcuttur. Sifirdir.

03

Auditable AI disclosureDenetlenebilir yapay zeka ifsasi

Every booklet carries a machine-readable block declaring the AI contribution level, the model, the human review state, and the verified and fabricated citation counts. Disclosure is auditable at the field level, not merely claimed.Her broşür, yapay zeka katki duzeyini, modeli, insan-inceleme durumunu ve dogrulanmis ile uydurma atif sayilarini beyan eden makine-okunur bir blok tasir. Ifsa, yalnizca iddia edilmez, alan duzeyinde denetlenebilir.

Disclosure and evidenceIfsa ve kanit

Rigorous enough to be a scholarly reference, not a promotional one.Tanitim degil, bilimsel bir referans olacak kadar titiz.

Built for the dominant use, answered to the named fear.Baskin kullanim icin kuruldu, adi konan korkuya cevap verdi.

A large 2026 survey of quantitative social scientists found that the dominant use of coding agents is data analysis, and that researchers fear AI worsening selective reporting. The data analysis booklets answer that fear directly, with reproducibility, pre-specification, and verification discipline.2026 tarihli buyuk olcekli bir nicel sosyal bilimci taramasi, coding agent'larin baskin kullaniminin veri analizi oldugunu ve arastirmacilarin yapay zekanin secici raporlamayi kotulestirmesinden korktugunu buldu. Veri analizi broşürleri bu korkuya yeniden-uretilebilirlik, on-belirtim ve dogrulama disipliniyle dogrudan cevap verir.
